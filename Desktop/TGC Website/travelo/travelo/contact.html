FAQ's

Do we have "Outdoor Toilets"?

Yes, We'll be fixing an utility tent away from the campsite which are comfortable for ladies & kids. We don't have a composting toilets as of now.

What kind of food is served at the campsite?

Food preferences (veg/non-veg) would be collected from everyone according to which food is served hot.Food for the kids below 5 years need to be carried by the parent.

Is water provided at the campsite?

Yes, We do provide mineral water for drinking. Any other preferenced water (boiled water) need to be carried by the gypsey.

What all things should be carried to the campsite?

Extra pair of shoes (trekking), jacket/hoodie, toiletries, extra pair of clother & blanket (optional).

What are the payment modes agreed by TGC?

We accept NEFT & UPI transfers, Google Pay and Paytm. Details would be provided by the TGC co-ordinator.
