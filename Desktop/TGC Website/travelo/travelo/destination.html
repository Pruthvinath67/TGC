Gandikota

“Live with no excuses and travel with no regrets”

Description

If you think adventure is dangerous, try routine. It’s lethal! We wait until we are ready, we will be waiting for the rest of our life! Life is an adventure and that is best lived boldly. Get ready for some action and join The Gypsey Co for memborable adventurous trip.

Day-01

Meeting point at The Swiss Castle, Attapur at 23:00hrs. A traveler/car would be arranged to start the trip. The trip starts by 23:20hrs post a small briefing regarding the trip.

Day-02

The first destination is Owk reservoir. Reach the destination by 06:30hrs. Sunrise from the view point. Can stay there till 08:00hrs. Move to the breakfast location from the reservoir.

Post breakfast, we will be travelling to Belum caves. The entry ticket is not included in the package. We can roam around the place for 90-120mins. By 12:00hrs start for Gandikota which is 60kms from Belum caves.

Reach Gandikota and get the accommodation (Dormitory). Freshen up and the homely lunch would be ready for you. A small trek to the Gandikota view point by 16:30hrs and can view the sunset at Gandikota.

By 19:00hrs reach the campus and freshen up. Get ready for a campfire and some entertainment in the campus. At 20:30hrs dinner is served at the campus. Complete your dinner and can go for a small walk inside the campus itself. By 22:00hrs need to go sleep as next day it very curial for us.

Day-03

Wake up at 05:30hrs and get ready for the morning sunrise hike and Gandikota tour which would be guided by our instructors. Once we are done with that we would be going for the breakfast where you would be served with dosa/idly.

Reach campus and get ready for the more excited activities that we offer like Rock Climbing, Rappelling, Hiking and ATV ride. We would be completing these activities by afternoon and coming back to the campus by the lunch time. Lunch would be served at the campus dinning. Once we are done with the lunch we would be heading to Yaganti Temple which is 85kms from Gandikota.

At Yaganti Temple we would be staying around 30mins and take Darshan of Lord Shiva. As this is the last place which is included in the package, once we are done with this we would be heading back to Hyderabad. There would be 2 breaks in the middle for evening snacks and dinner which are not included in the package.

Dropping point would be at Mehdipatnam.
